Welcome to the Repeater page.


The CARC owns and maintains two repeaters. One on 145.310 - .6 offset with a PL of 103.5 as well as one on 443.625 + 5 with a PL of 97.4.


Orginally the plan was to put on the air on repeater. From a older wibsite published in 2014, the 2 meter frequency used was 146.730 -. We tried to get coordinated for this frequency, but there was a repeater just too close (85 miles. 90 miles is the Arkansas Repeater Council minimum).
